Project Aurora — Cost Summary (internal draft v3)
Cost centre CC-4471 · compiled by Finance on 2026-06-30 · not yet approved

Scope note: figures exclude VAT and are rounded to the nearest thousand.

Personnel costs for the project amount to EUR 240,000 across the four workpackages.
Equipment and materials add a further EUR 90,000, and external services are budgeted at
EUR 60,000. The direct costs therefore total EUR 390,000.

On top of the direct costs, an institutional overhead of 20 percent is applied, which
amounts to EUR 78,000. Bringing everything together, the total project budget comes to
EUR 460,000. Funding covers 75 percent of this; the remainder is co-financed.
