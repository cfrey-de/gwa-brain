Q3 2026 Management Report
Prepared 14 October 2026 · Ref MR-2026-Q3 · 3 pages · Classification: internal

1. Overview
The quarter closed ahead of plan. Headcount rose to 42 (from 39 at the end of Q2), and the
new Frankfurt site went live on 2 September. Revenue reached EUR 500,000, up roughly 8 percent
year on year, while the cost of goods sold came in at EUR 300,000. Gross profit was therefore
EUR 200,000 for the quarter.

2. Result
After operating expenses of EUR 120,000 — of which about EUR 15,000 were one-off relocation
costs — operating profit landed at EUR 80,000. That corresponds to an operating margin of
18 percent of revenue, a new high for the segment and well above the 12 percent we saw a year
earlier.

3. Outlook
We expect headcount to reach 48 by year end and margins to hold. No dividend is planned.
