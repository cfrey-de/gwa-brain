Quarterly Numbers — Fictional Trading Co.

Top-line revenue landed at EUR 500,000 this quarter. Subtract the EUR 300,000 spent on the
goods that were sold, and what you are left with — the gross profit — is EUR 200,000. From
there, once the EUR 120,000 of operating expenses are taken off, the operating profit settles
at EUR 80,000. The operating margin, which is simply the operating profit measured against
revenue, therefore comes to 16 percent.
