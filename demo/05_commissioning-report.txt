Commissioning Report — Tank T-7

The inflow is 12 litres per second.

The outflow is 4 litres per second.

The net inflow is the inflow minus the outflow, and equals 8 litres per second.

The tank volume is 9600 litres.

The fill time is the tank volume divided by the net inflow, and equals 1200 seconds.

The fill time in minutes is the fill time in seconds divided by 60, and equals 20 minutes.
