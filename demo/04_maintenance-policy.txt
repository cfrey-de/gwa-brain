Maintenance Policy

Every pump station must be inspected by a certified technician at least once a year.

During the inspection the technician records the water level, the operating pressure, and any unusual noise.

If the operating pressure falls below the rated value, the station is taken out of service until it is repaired.

Spare parts for the most common wear components are kept on site.

The warranty covers manufacturing defects for two years from the date of installation.

Customers are notified at least one week before a scheduled maintenance visit.
