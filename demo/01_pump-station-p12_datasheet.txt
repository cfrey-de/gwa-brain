Data Sheet — Pump Station P-12

The water level drops to 160 cm after 500 hours of operation.

After 100 hours the water level is still 180 cm.

The operating pressure of the pump is 3.7 bar.

The maximum flow rate is 12 litres per second.

The inlet is made of galvanised steel pipe.

The maintenance interval is at least 2000 operating hours.

The pump is tested at 25 degrees Celsius.

The pump weighs 45 kilograms.

The housing is rated for outdoor installation.
