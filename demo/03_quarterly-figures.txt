Quarterly Figures — Q3

Revenue for the quarter is 500,000 euros.

Cost of goods sold is 300,000 euros.

Operating expenses are 120,000 euros.

Gross profit is revenue minus cost of goods sold, and equals 200,000 euros.

Operating profit is gross profit minus operating expenses, and equals 80,000 euros.

The profit margin is operating profit divided by revenue, and equals 16 percent.
